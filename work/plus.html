1. Project Overview

What is PLUS?

PLUS (Personalized Learning Squared) is a CMU-based startup intent on doubling math learning for 10k+ middle schoolers by providing novel human-AI tutoring opportunities to under-served communities.

Currently, PLUS has helped 3k+ students through 500+ tutors, and has conducted 10k+ total hours of tutoring.

My Role

Based on user interviews from tutors and lead tutors, I transitioned an external tutor scheduling Google spreadsheet to a native PLUS app scheduling table.

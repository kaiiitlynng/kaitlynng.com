1. Project Overview

What is PLUS?

PLUS (Personalized Learning Squared) is a startup backed by CMU and Stanford University intent on doubling math learning for middle schoolers by providing novel human-AI tutoring opportunities to under-served communities. PLUS is a part of the Simon Initiative.

My Role

I conducted a UI/UX facelift of the DataShop website as my first project under PLUS. My design went live on June 2nd, 2024. Visit the new and improved DataShop here.
